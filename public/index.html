🤝 What Is This?

This is an AI-powered coaching conversation — not therapy, not advice, not consulting.

A coach helps you think more clearly by listening deeply, reflecting back what you say, and asking questions that open up new perspectives.

What to expect:

  • No advice — You'll be asked questions, not told what to do
  • Your words — The coach mirrors your language, not its own
  • One question at a time — Space to think without overwhelm
  • Your pace — Go wherever feels most useful to you

Good starting points:

  • A decision you're weighing
  • A goal you want to clarify
  • Something you feel stuck on
  • A situation you'd like to see differently

⚠️ Important

This is an AI coaching assistant, not a human coach or therapist.

Please note: This assistant follows ICF (International Coaching Federation) coaching guidelines. It will not diagnose, give medical advice, or provide therapy. If you are in crisis or need clinical support, please contact a qualified professional.

  • Not a substitute for professional mental health support
  • AI-generated responses may not always be perfect
  • Your conversation is stored locally in your browser only
